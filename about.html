Our Mission

At SwiftNav Logistics, we believe that global supply chains shouldn't be a black box. Our mission is to provide transparent, reliable, and hyper-efficient freight forwarding solutions to businesses of all sizes. Built on modern technology and decades of logistics expertise, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time.

Whether it's an urgent air freight transfer, a cost-effective ocean container, or dependable last-mile ground transport, our dedicated team works around the clock to navigate the complexities of international trade so you can focus on growing your business.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At SwiftNav Logistics, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide logistics services, including names, contact details, and shipping addresses.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to facilitate shipping, provide tracking updates, and improve our services. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers (Supabase/PostgreSQL) to protect your sensitive information.

4. Your Rights

You can request access to or deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.
